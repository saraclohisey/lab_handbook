# Introduction

The viva is incredibly stressful for the Vivee (made up word). Although we mostly know everything will be ok, until the viva is over *they* will not feel that way! Taking some of the stress away is helpful. So try to take most of the organisation away (unless they are a natural organiser and this is how they relax!)

Please note that tea/coffee is an important part of the viva. It can provide a natural break and breather during a very intense time. 

Not only that but the student will need sustenance (sugar) so they can maintain their energy levels. Most examiners will try to complete a viva in a reasonable amount of time but others will just keep going....
